    Do not decide from adrenaline

    An offer can feel urgent and emotional. The safer move is to separate excitement, pressure, open questions, and actual fit before responding.

    Ask for one clean next step

    The best response usually clarifies one thing: the role, the terms, the timeline, the growth path, or the decision window.
