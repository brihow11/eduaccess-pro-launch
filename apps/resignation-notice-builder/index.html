    Keep it short

    The safest resignation note usually states the decision, gives timing, says thank you, and offers a practical transition step.

    Leave private details out

    This public page gives a message frame only. Employer names, manager names, salary numbers, start dates, workplace complaints, and private reasons should stay out of this tool.
