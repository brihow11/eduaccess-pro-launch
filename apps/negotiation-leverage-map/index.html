    Leverage is not pressure

    A useful negotiation connects the ask to value, fit, scope, timing, or risk reduction. It should not sound like a threat.

    Ask for one thing first

    The safest first move is usually one focused change: compensation, title/scope, start date, decision window, or clarity on terms.
